## Methodology

Keypoints:
1. Paragraph 1
- Provide a general introduction and overview of the material/methods
- Restate the purpose of the work
- Give the source of materials/equipment
- Suppy essential background information

2. Paragraph 2
- Provide specific and precise details about materials and methods (i.e quantities, temperatures, duration, sequence, conditions, locations, sizes)
- Justify choice made
- Idicate that appropriate care was taken

3. Paragraph 3
- Relate materials/methods to other studies
- Idicate where the problem occurred
